Our Heritage

A Legacy Born in
Khalilabad

Deep in the heartland of Uttar Pradesh, in the ancient town of Khalilabad, the Sharma family has been crafting pickles using time-honoured recipes for over three generations. Every jar carries the essence of this land — its sunlight, its soil, and its soul.

Our dadi's kitchen was always fragrant with mustard seeds crackling in oil, raw mangoes being sliced with precision, and the earthy warmth of hand-ground masalas. Those flavours became our identity. Sharma Acharnama™ is our tribute to her — a promise to keep her recipes alive.

"जो स्वाद घर में है, वो बाजार में नहीं मिलता — इसीलिए हम घर का स्वाद आप तक लाते हैं।"

— Durgesh Sharma, Founder

Our Craft

From Farm to Your Table

Every pickle jar is a result of a meticulous, love-filled process that honours both tradition and quality.

🌱
Fresh Ingredient Selection
We source the finest raw mangoes, lemons, chillis, and garlic directly from local UP farmers at the peak of their season — freshness guaranteed.

Traditional Spice Preparation
Whole spices — methi, kalonji, saunf, rai — are dry-roasted and ground fresh on sil-batta. No packaged masala ever touches our pickles.

Meet the Founder

Durgesh
Sharma

Founder & Visionary · Sharma Acharnama™

Born and raised in the cultural heartland of Khalilabad, Uttar Pradesh, Durgesh Sharma grew up watching his dadi and mother craft the most soul-satisfying pickles — each jar a labour of love, each recipe a family secret.

Armed with a passion for India's culinary heritage and a modern entrepreneurial vision, Durgesh founded Sharma Acharnama™ to bridge the gap between authentic homemade flavours and India's growing appetite for real, pure food. His mission: put the taste of every Indian grandmother's kitchen on every Indian family's table.

Today, Sharma Acharnama™ is more than a brand — it is a movement to celebrate and preserve the culinary traditions of rural India.

Village Origin

Rooted in the
Heart of UP

Where ancient traditions meet the fertile plains of the Ganga basin

Khalilabad is one of UP's oldest towns — a place where time moves differently, where neighbours share food freely, and where the art of making achaar is considered as sacred as prayer. The town's fertile land, clean air, and warm sun create the perfect environment for our pickles to mature.

The Sharma family home in Khalilabad is where every jar begins its journey — from the first slice of a fresh mango to the last seal of a filled glass jar. When you open a Sharma Acharnama™ product, you open a window into this beautiful world.
